Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program for?

This program is specifically designed for final year BTech students who want to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements. It's ideal for students looking to enhance their practical skills and secure good job placements.

What is the time commitment required?

The program runs on weekends over 16 weeks (4 months). Each weekend includes full-day sessions on both Saturday and Sunday. Some additional time during weekdays is recommended for project work and practice.

Do I need prior programming experience?

Basic programming knowledge is expected, but the program starts with fundamentals and gradually builds to more advanced concepts. Even students with minimal programming background can catch up with some additional effort.

How is this different from online courses?

Unlike most online courses, this program focuses on applied learning through real-world projects, includes direct mentorship, provides mock interviews, and offers personalized career guidance. The emphasis is on creating a portfolio and being job-ready, not just acquiring theoretical knowledge.

Will there be placement assistance?

While we don't guarantee placements, the program equips you with everything needed to succeed in tech interviews. Our career preparation includes resume building, LinkedIn optimization, mock interviews, and application strategies that significantly improve your chances of securing good job offers.
